🚀 Just launched: Compliance-Llama! 🦙

I'm incredibly excited to share a project that tackles one of the biggest bottlenecks in MedTech: regulatory documentation. 

Compliance-Llama is an audit-ready SOP generator that takes a simple device description and outputs a fully structured QMS artifact, complete with clause-by-clause citations for ISO 13485:2016, 21 CFR Part 820, and even advanced cybersecurity requirements (FDA 2023, ISO 27001). 

But here is the coolest part: the entire architecture, synthetic data generation, and advanced regulatory scenario integration was built and orchestrated using the newly released **Gemini 3 Pro CLI**! 🤯 

The speed, context awareness, and coding capabilities of Gemini are absolutely game-changing. It allowed me to map out complex regulatory frameworks and synthesize advanced compliance data in a fraction of the time it would normally take.
